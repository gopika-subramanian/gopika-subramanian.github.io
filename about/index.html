About Me

Gopika is a Computer Science and Engineering student at Amrita Viswa Vidhyapeetham, Amritapuri, India, a member of team bi0s which is the #1 CTF team of India, and team shakti #1 Women team in India, actively taking part in the international CTFs round the year, besides carrying out research on security issues in the real world where she specialise in Web Exploitation, Web Security and Database Exploitation. Her interest in security motivated her to participate in Capture the Flag style security contests which enabled her to adopt secure coding practices and understand different types of vulnerabilities, how to exploit them and more importantly how to fix them.
